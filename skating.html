Private Skating Tutor

In high school, I was a private skating tutor for young children, aged around 4-6.
When I was younger, I trained as a figure skater. Out of passion, I picked it back up (recreationally) in high school. This is where I started a small ice skating tutoring business that allowed me to apply my muscle memory of basic skills and teach them to beginner skaters.
Being a tutor to new and young skaters came with many challenges. In addition to teaching the basics of skating (i.e. stopping, form, backwards skating, etc.), I had to adapt to a new method of communication to a unique audience.
In an environment in which both the teacher and learner can be easily frustrated, I was consistently tested on my patience, perseverance, and persistence.
