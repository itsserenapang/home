Courage

Teaming up with a Queen's Commerce Student, I built Courage out of a place of love. The idea was grown from a local store in Kensington Market, that inpired me so much that it made me become passionate about supporting the needs of local businesses and communities during this difficult time.

Courage, My Love is one of those small businesses that carry an experience and a story with their merchandise. The store, cluttered with various treasurers makes you feel as if you've stepped into another world. From WW2 Era jewelry to self-dyed cashmere, Courage, My Love embodies the idea of a hometown store that would take you in during times of need.
