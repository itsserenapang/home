Courage

Teaming up with a Queen's Commerce Student, I built Courage out of a local business in Kensington Market, that inpired me so much that it impassioned me to be more active in supporting the needs of community sellers during this difficult time.

Courage, My Love is one of those small businesses that create a second home of warmth for their customers out of their small business. They're one of those inspiring stores that you know would welcome anyone in their times of need.
