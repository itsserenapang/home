Courage

Teaming up with a Queen's Commerce Student, I built Courage out of a local business in Kensington Market, that inpired me so much that it impassioned me to be more active in supporting the needs of community sellers during this difficult time.

Courage, My Love is one of those small businesses that create a second home of warmth for their customers out of their small business. They're one of those inspiring stores that you know would welcome anyone in their times of need.

  • Learn About Courage

The Problem

As an avid seeker for unique retail experiences, small businesses have always been my priority when shopping. However, with recent developments in our world, they need to be provided a low-cost, unified, platform to improve engagement during points of economic downturn.
My solution is Courage. Courage is an app specifically made to facilitate small businesses and their business ventures. Not only does it provide a convenient shopping experience, but also allows shoppers and retailers to connect, reminding us of the communities we support when we invest in local businesses.

Consumer Perspective

Small businesses rely on their relationship with the consumer. To improve the customer experience, we've built a comprehensive questionnaire to help pinpoint what stores to recommend new users to.

We believe that shopping with a small business should inspire communities. That's why we tailor your suggestions based on accumulated data of your interactions on our app. With Courage, not only will you enjoy the product, but you'll also know the stories of the people that brought it to you.


Retailer Perspective

Small businesses are the backbones of many communities. With an increasing demand for retail with online accessibility, local stores struggle to find a cheap, unified platform suited for their business.
With Courage, we make the process of transitioning simple by connecting retailers with shoppers with similar interests.

We kept Courage's design simple and straight-forward, with it being easily usable for any age range. With the dwindling number of local businesses, we would not want to create an unnecessarily difficult navigation when many would instead be helping their community come together.
