Royal Canadian Air Cadets, Side Projects

In my 5 years in the cadet program, I participated in a lot of teams within the organization. Ranging from Ontario-Level Cadet Media and PR to Public Speaking, I was extremely active in the program.
As a senior in the program, you're expected to be involved. Since my passions were in design, I started with our local Media and PR Team, later working my way up to various other clubs and positions.

  • Learn About My Squadron

8 Globemaster Squadron

In Media Team, I was the President for 3 years. I managed our social media pages, postings on our websites, create a database with all the cadets in it, and created our end of the year annual booklet.
Eventually, I became one of the main contributors to the Ontario Cadets page, as the cadet correspondent for the Central Ontario Area (COA).

Furthering my skills, I became the President of our squadron's Public Speaking Team for 2 years. Working with the Toastmasters Program. I coordinated with 3 squadrons to create an environment for learning from Toastmaster competitors, and managed weekly meetings during the duration of the training session.
